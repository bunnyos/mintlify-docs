Your [actions](/user/actions/builder) run around the clock and post what they find into the **actions inbox**: a feed of signals you can read at a glance and act on without leaving the page. The inbox is one of three views you switch between at the top of the Actions tab: **inbox** (this page), **builder**, and **history**. New signals appear on their own as your actions run.

<Note>
  The inbox shows what your own actions produce, plus recommendations from creators whose keys you hold. To create the actions that fill it, see [Create actions that run 24/7](/user/actions/builder). To manage existing ones, see [Manage your actions](/user/actions/management).
</Note>

## Alerts, recommendations, and creator picks

The inbox splits signals into three columns. On a wide screen they sit side by side; on a narrow screen they stack into one scrolling list. Each column header shows how many pending items it holds.

| Column | What it is | Can you execute it? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **your actions** | Suggested moves from your own actions. | Yes, one click |
| **from creators** | Picks and calls from creators whose keys you hold. | Plain-language calls only; on-chain calls are view only |
| **alerts** | Heads-up only, no action required. Warnings and info your actions post when conditions trigger. | No, informational |

A colored dot marks each card's kind: **alerts** are yellow (attention) and **recommendations** are green (actionable). Think of alerts as "something happened, look at it" and recommendations as "here is a move you can make right now."

<Tip>
  The **from creators** column only fills once your wallet is connected to Bunny Exchange. If it is empty and shows a **go to bunny exchange** button, connect first. See [Connect your wallet](/user/getting-started/wallet-connection) and [Buy & sell bunny keys](/user/bunnyex/trading).
</Tip>

## Reading a card

Every card has the same layout so you can scan it fast:

- A **title**: the one-line summary of the signal.
- The **source**: which of your actions raised it.
- A **timestamp** like "just now", "5m ago", or "2h ago"; hover it to see the exact time.
- A short **description** with the detail behind the signal.
- **Tokens mentioned**, when the signal involves specific tokens. Each token chip shows its symbol and a shortened address; click a chip to copy the full address.

Executable recommendations may also show an **execute** preview, which is the exact instruction that will be handed to chat when you act.

## Acting on a card

<Steps>
  <Step title="Execute a recommendation">
    Click **execute** on a recommendation from your own actions. It opens chat and drops the recommendation's instructions into the composer, ready to send.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute a creator pick">
    Cards in **from creators** that carry a plain-language call also show **execute**, which fills chat the same way. On-chain calls are view only: read them, then decide how to act yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hide what you have handled">
    Click **hide** on a card from your own actions or an alert to clear it from the live inbox. Hidden items are restorable: they stay in **history** with an **unhide** button. Use **hide all** in the header to clear every pending card at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dismiss a creator call">
    Creator cards use **dismiss** instead of hide. Dismissing is permanent: it removes the call from your feed for good, with no undo, and sends nothing to chat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Executing never moves funds by itself: it only pre-fills the chat composer. You send the message, and you approve every on-chain transaction in your [connected wallet](/user/getting-started/wallet-connection). Review the instructions before you run them.
</Warning>

## The history view

Switch to the **history** view to see everything your actions have ever posted: pending, executed, and hidden, newest first and grouped by day (**today**, **yesterday**, then dated headers). The header shows a running tally, for example "4 pending, 12 executed, 7 hidden".

Two filter rows let you narrow the list:

| Filter | Options |
| --- | --- |
| **kind** | all, recommendations, alerts |
| **status** | all, pending, executed, hidden |

Each history card repeats the title, kind, status, time, source, description, any tokens, and the original execute instruction. Hidden cards appear dimmed and carry an **unhide** button; click it to restore the item so it shows up under **inbox** again.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why did a recommendation disappear before I acted on it?">
    Creator picks drop off the feed once they are a few days old, so stale calls do not pile up. Your own actions' recommendations and alerts stay until you hide them, and remain visible in history afterward.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I hid something by mistake, can I get it back?">
    Yes. Open **history**, find the dimmed card, and click **unhide**. (Creator **dismiss** actions are the exception: they cannot be restored.)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why does a card show no execute button?">
    Alerts are informational and never have one. For creator picks, see the view-only rule above: only plain-language calls are executable.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
