---
title: "Track your P&L on bunny keys"
description: "See your realized, unrealized, and total P&L across your bunny keys."
---

Every key you buy or sell on bunnyDEX is tracked so you can see how each position is doing. Once you connect your bunnyOS account, a **P&L** card appears above your trade list and shows where your position stands right now. Open a bunny from the list and scroll to the `your trades` section in its detail view to find it. All amounts are shown in `$OS`, with gains in green and losses in red.

<Note>
The P&L card is not shown until you connect your bunnyOS account. See [Connect bunnyOS to see your P&L](#connect-bunnyos-to-see-your-pl) below to surface it. P&L covers keys you traded through bunnyDEX while signed in with your Base wallet. See [Buying and selling keys](/user/bunnyex/trading) to start a position, and [Connect your wallet](/user/getting-started/wallet-connection) if you haven't linked a Base wallet yet.
</Note>

## Per-bunny metrics

For a single bunny, the P&L card reports:

| Metric | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| `realized` | Locked-in profit or loss from keys you have already sold. |
| `unrealized` | Paper profit or loss on the keys you still hold, valued at the current price. |
| `total p&l` | The headline figure: your overall profit or loss on this bunny. |

`total p&l` is the headline figure. A leading `+` and green text mean you are up, and red means you are down. For `you own` and `your position`, see the `bunny details` column on [Buying and selling keys](/user/bunnyex/trading).

## Connect bunnyOS to see your P&L

The P&L card appears once you connect your bunnyOS account, so connecting is what surfaces your full P&L breakdown across your positions. It reuses your existing Base wallet to sign in, so there is no separate password or new wallet to set up.

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect your wallet">
Make sure you are signed in with your Base wallet first. See [Connect your wallet](/user/getting-started/wallet-connection).
</Step>
<Step title="Connect bunnyOS">
Open the [bunnyOS account](/user/configure/bunnyos-account) page and start the connect flow. You will approve a one-time signature in your wallet to link your account.
</Step>
<Step title="See your full P&L">
Return to a bunny's `your trades` section. With bunnyOS connected, the P&L card shows your realized, unrealized, and total figures.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
A trade you just submitted appears in your history and P&L only once it has confirmed. If a fresh buy or sell isn't reflected yet, give it a moment and refresh.
</Warning>
