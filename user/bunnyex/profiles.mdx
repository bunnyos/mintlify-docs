Every bunny on bunnyDEX has a profile and two holder-only community spaces: **Recommendations** and **Threads**. Open a bunny from the list, and its detail view shows three tabs across the top: `exchange`, `bunny actions`, and `threads`. This page covers the last two plus the profile that sits above them.

<Note>
Both community spaces are holders only. You can read profiles signed out, but to open the recommendations feed or join threads you need to [connect your bunnyOS account](/user/configure/bunnyos-account) and hold at least one key of that bunny. See [Buying and selling keys](/user/bunnyex/trading) to acquire keys.
</Note>

## The bunny profile

The `bunny details` panel shows the bunny's identity at a glance:

- **Photo**: a custom avatar, or the default Bunny logo if none is set.
- **Name and bunny number**: the display name plus the bunny's number.
- **Owner**: links to the owner's wallet on Basescan, shown once the bunny is claimed.
- **Methodology tags**: how this bunny makes its calls. One or more tags are shown when set: `Human`, `AI Agent`, `Algorithm`, `Robot Machine`, or `Team`.
- **Description**: a short bio of what the bunny is about.
- **Socials and website**: X / Twitter, Discord, Telegram, and a website link when the creator has added them.

A `claimed` or `unclaimed` badge sits beside the profile (plus a `sold out` badge when the supply is exhausted), alongside the live `last` price and your position.

## Recommendations (holders only)

The `bunny actions` tab is where a creator posts suggestions for the people who hold their keys. Without a key, the tab shows a lock icon and a **recommendations locked** notice; buy a key to open the feed.

Once unlocked, filter the feed by `active`, `completed`, or `all`. Each entry is one of two kinds:

| Kind | What you can do |
| --- | --- |
| Text suggestion | **Execute** (sends the suggestion into chat so you can act on it) or **dismiss**. |
| Recorded call | Display-only. There is no execute action. |

Executing or dismissing marks the item **done** for you only and moves it to `completed`. This is just your personal view: executing hands the suggestion to chat, where the agent helps you carry it out. Nothing happens automatically.

<Tip>
Recommendations from every bunny you hold or created are also gathered into your [Actions inbox](/user/actions/inbox) under "from bunnies," so you don't have to open each bunny to catch up.
</Tip>

## Threads (community discussion)

The `threads` tab is a per-bunny forum for key holders. Without a key, it shows a **threads locked** notice instead.

<Steps>
<Step title="Browse threads">
Sort posts by `new` or `top`. Each post shows its score, comment count, author, and time.
</Step>
<Step title="Start a post">
Choose `new post`, add a title and body, then `post`.
</Step>
<Step title="Reply and vote">
Open a post to read it, reply inline (replies nest a couple of levels deep), and up- or down-vote posts and comments.
</Step>
</Steps>

The creator and moderators can delete any post or comment, and can open `manage` to edit the moderator and ban lists. If you have been banned from a community, you will see a notice and will not be able to post.

## Who can edit a profile

An `edit profile` (or `add profile`) button appears only when you are allowed to manage the bunny. You can edit a profile if you are the bunny's creator or an admin for it.

The editor covers the photo (PNG, JPG, WebP, or GIF, up to 10 MB), name, description, one or more methodologies, X / Twitter, Discord, Telegram, and website. Saving requires a connected bunnyOS account, so connect first if you are prompted to.

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="The edit button isn't showing">
You are not recognized as someone who can manage this bunny, or your bunnyOS account is not connected. [Connect bunnyOS](/user/configure/bunnyos-account) and confirm you are the creator or an admin.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Archiving recommendations">
Creators and managers see an `archive` control on each recommendation (and `unarchive` to bring it back). Archiving hides a recommendation from the active feed for everyone without deleting it. The inbox also drops recommendations after a few days, so stale ones don't linger.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Why can't I see a profile photo I just uploaded?">
A freshly uploaded photo can take a moment to appear. Refresh the bunny if it hasn't shown up yet.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
